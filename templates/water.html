Activating Melbourne water land on the Mornington Peninsula


Focusing on the Mornington Peninsula, this map shows Melbourne Water land in relation to the Mornington Peninsula walking trail. This land (shown on the map in bright green, and also clickable) bisects the area that the walking trail follows. An opportunity to activate this land is to create a connecting walking trail with the Mornington Peninsula trail. Shorter walking trails give more opportunity to locals and visitors to the area be active, and add variety.

Working with the Shire of Mornington Peninsula, local schools and other community groups, Melbourne Water can activate this land to be a walking trail and green space. Planting native vegetation along a walking trail would provide habitat for native birds and animals. This project would benefit of the health and wellbeing of the local community and visitors.
