Our mission

Greening Space aims to convert the concrete jungle into a greener and more open community space. Within the metropolitan area our aim is to map unloved public spaces in the City of Melbourne to enable the community to find spaces to grow and cultivate community gardens. Within the state of Victoria we want to use Melbourne Water land to create more green spaces within the State.

Data used to achieve this goal

Greening Space Melbourne uses interdisciplinary data to model and help the City of Melbourne and the community understand urban green systems and the social and environmental that are interwoven within them. The data sets used include community data such as open spaces, existing gardens in addition to environmental data including the tree plantation data within the city and biodiversity density.
