Making public spaces that are greener and cleaner.


Greening space is about activating unloved spaces by opening access to prospective community gardens. We locate potential area for new pop up gardens, to beautify the area, to provide food locally and to create community around cultivating produce. We connect with schools, social enterprise and community organisations to maintain or upkeep the gardens for public use.
